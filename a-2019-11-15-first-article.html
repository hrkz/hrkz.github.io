A first article about the aims of this blog

This short article is just describing what this blog will be about and what are the plans for the future. I don't want to follow a regular posting time, since my schedule can vary a lot. It is possible that some months will see no post while other will have plenty. Also, I encourage you to contact me by email if you have any remarks / questions / ideas.

1. Imagine

I am working on a symbolic mathematics library in Rust which goal is oriented toward numerical analysis (discretisation of differential equations, transformation to variational form, etc.). This blog will mostly be following the different milestones of this development and talk about the problems that I have been facing and how I solved them (if I did).
The project is available on Github under MIT licence and will be the subject of a next article. At the moment, it only contains basic features such as:
  • Creation and simplification of algebraic expressions.
  • Printing in different forms, including expression-tree and LaTeX standard.
  • Basic polynomial support.

2. Physics topics from a mathematical point of view

I will sometimes try to explain how an observed or measured physical phenomena can be turned into an equation and how a numerical model would be able to reproduce it.

3. Tips and tricks

I like to share some tips and tricks that I found during my work, whether it is about scientific writing, coding or anything else.
